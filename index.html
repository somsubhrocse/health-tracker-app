🌿 The MediPrep Plan
🍋

A sustainable approach to fat loss.

This plan is designed for busy professionals who want to drop body fat and preserve muscle, but don't have the time to cook every single meal. By combining the heart-healthy principles of the Mediterranean diet with a strategic batch-prep routine, you can eat beautifully without the daily stress.

The Core Pillars

🥩

High Protein Focus

To preserve muscle mass while in a calorie deficit, every meal is anchored by a strong protein source—ranging from Skyr and eggs to lean turkey, salmon, and plant-based legumes.

⏱️

The 6:00 PM Hunger Buster

The post-work hunger spike is where most diets fail. We actively combat this by scheduling a mandatory, high-protein snack right as the workday ends to stabilize blood sugar before dinner.

🥗

Vegetarian Workdays

To boost fiber intake and ensure lunches are easy to pack and store safely at the office, all weekday lunches are 100% vegetarian, relying on robust ingredients like quinoa and chickpeas.
